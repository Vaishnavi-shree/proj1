🛡 Prevention of Social Engineering Attacks

Home Types Safety Tips Prevention Case Studies

👥 User Education

Conduct regular awareness sessions and phishing simulations to train employees and users.

🔍 Verification Process

Always verify requests for sensitive information. Call back or confirm the identity through another channel.

🔐 Security Policies

Implement and enforce policies such as no password sharing, screen locking, and secure communication.

🧠 Psychological Awareness

Educate users on tactics like urgency, fear, or curiosity that attackers use to manipulate behavior.

🛡️ Email and Spam Filters

Use robust email filters and anti-virus software to detect and block malicious messages.
